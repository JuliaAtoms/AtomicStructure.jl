    Hydrogenic initialization

    Hydrogenic initialization

    Atoms.hydrogenic! — Function.
    hydrogenic!(atom[; kwargs...])

    Initialize the radial orbitals of atom to their unscreened hydrogenic values. This is done via simple diagonalization of the one-body Hamiltonian for each angular symmetry. The kwargs are passed on to diagonalize_one_body and can be used to influence how the diagonalization is performed.
